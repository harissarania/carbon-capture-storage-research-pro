Project scope
In scope
  • Literature review & sorbent screening
  • Bench-scale & pilot-scale capture testing
  • Geological site survey & storage modelling
  • HSE assessment
  • Regulatory review & EIA
  • Economic feasibility model
Out of scope
  • Full commercial plant construction
  • CO₂ utilization or conversion research
  • Transportation pipeline design

Key assumptions & constraints
Regulatory permits assumed within 90 days of project start. Geological survey partner must be identified by Month 3. Pilot storage site must comply with ISO 27914. Budget is fixed — no scope changes after Phase 1 gate review. Research output must be suitable for peer-reviewed publication.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

1. Evaluate and select the optimal CO₂ absorbent (amine, ionic liquid, or MOF)
2. Design and construct a bench-scale capture unit (Phase 1)
3. Scale to a pilot plant with ≥ 90% CO₂ capture efficiency (Phase 2)
4. Assess geological storage site suitability via seismic and core analysis
5. Conduct an Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and regulatory feasibility review
6. Deliver a go/no-go recommendation for commercial-scale deployment

## ✅ Success Criteria

| Criterion | Target |
|---|---|
| CO₂ capture efficiency (pilot scale) | ≥ 90% |
| Levelized cost of capture | ≤ RM 60 / tonne CO₂ |
| Lost-time incidents (LTI) | **Zero** — zero-tolerance policy |
| Milestones delivered on schedule | ≥ 90% |
| Budget variance | ≤ ±5% of approved budget |
| Geological storage capacity confirmed | ≥ 1 Mt CO₂ |

| Sorbent cycle lifetime | ≥ 1,000 cycles |

## 🗂️ Project Scope

### ✅ In Scope
- Literature review and sorbent screening
- Bench-scale and pilot-scale capture testing
- Geological site survey and storage capacity modelling
- Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) assessment
- Regulatory review and Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
- Economic feasibility model (levelized cost of capture)

### ❌ Out of Scope
- Full commercial plant construction
- CO₂ utilization or conversion research
- Transportation pipeline design

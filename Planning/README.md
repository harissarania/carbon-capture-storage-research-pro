## 🔬 Project Overview

The **CCS Research Initiative** is an 18-month applied research project aimed at evaluating the technical and economic feasibility of Carbon Capture & Storage (CCS) technology at pilot scale. The project progresses through two main technical phases — bench-scale R&D and pilot plant scale-up — alongside a parallel geological storage assessment, culminating in a board-ready go/no-go recommendation for commercial-scale development.

### 🎯 Project Objectives

| # | Objective |
|---|-----------|
| 1 | Achieve ≥ **90% CO₂ capture efficiency** at pilot scale |
| 2 | Identify optimal sorbent achieving ≤ **3.5 GJ/tonne CO₂** regeneration energy |
| 3 | Confirm geological storage capacity ≥ **1 Mt CO₂** |
| 4 | Deliver levelized cost of capture ≤ **RM 60/tonne** |
| 5 | Complete with **zero lost-time HSE incidents** |
| 6 | Produce a final CCS feasibility report with commercial go/no-go decision by **Month 18** |
